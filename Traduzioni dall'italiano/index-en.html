Instructions for home services (Gas)

This is the gas tap and it is located in the kitchen. When it is in the vertical position it means that the gas is not in operation, while when it is in the horizontal position, the gas is in operation.
Before using the kitchen or other services that require the use of gas, you must make sure that this is open.

Instructions for home services (Water heater)

Before using the hot water, you must make sure that the water heater is switched on. If the latter is off, it can be turned on from the appropriate button on the bathroom wall.

Separate collection of garbage

You will absolutely have to carry out separate collection. In the house you will find the collection methods and the calendar.
Only after dinner time will you have to leave the bins with the correct rubbish outside the driveway, near the roadside, and in the morning the ecological operators will come by to collect the garbage.
When the operators have finished picking up the garbage, you can take the basket home.
At check out you will have to take out all the garbage (which will not be picked up by the ecological operators) and you will have to go to the local ecological center whose location we provide you with.

Ecological island for departing tourists

In the months of July and August in the locality of Le Saline it will be set up an island manned only for outgoing utilities. The times of opening will be from 8:00 to 10:00 and from 16:00 to 18:00 every day of the week.
